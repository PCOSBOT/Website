Cardiovascular Exercise

Cardio exercise essential untuk meningkatkan insulin sensitivity dan membakar kalori. Recommended 150 menit per minggu dengan intensitas moderate.

Manfaat Cardio untuk PCOS:

Meningkatkan uptake glucose oleh otot, menurunkan insulin resistance, dan membantu weight loss yang sustainable.

Best Cardio Options:

  • Brisk walking 30-45 menit daily
  • Swimming untuk low-impact full body workout
  • Cycling untuk strengthen lower body
  • Dancing untuk fun dan sustainable exercise
  • Elliptical machine untuk joint-friendly option

Intensity Guidelines:

  • Moderate intensity: 50-70% max heart rate
  • Can hold conversation while exercising
  • Start with 20 minutes, gradually increase
üí™

Strength Training

Resistance training crucial untuk building lean muscle mass, boosting metabolism, dan improving insulin sensitivity long-term.

Key Benefits:

  • Increases muscle mass ‚Üí higher metabolism
  • Improves bone density (important untuk PCOS)
  • Enhances insulin sensitivity
  • Boosts confidence dan body image
  • Long-term metabolic benefits

Strength Training Schedule:

2-3 sessions per week, targeting all major muscle groups. Allow 48 hours recovery between sessions untuk same muscle groups.

Exercise Types:

  • Bodyweight exercises (squats, push-ups, planks)
  • Free weights (dumbbells, kettlebells)
  • Resistance bands untuk home workouts
  • Machine exercises untuk beginners

High-Intensity Interval Training

HIIT proven sangat efektif untuk PCOS management, combining cardio dan strength benefits dalam shorter time frame.

HIIT Benefits untuk PCOS:

  • Superior insulin sensitivity improvement
  • Efficient fat burning dan EPOC effect
  • Time-efficient (20-30 minutes)
  • Improves cardiovascular fitness rapidly
  • Can be modified untuk all fitness levels

HIIT Structure:

Alternate between high-intensity bursts (80-90% effort) for 20-60 seconds dengan recovery periods (50% effort) for equal atau longer duration.

Sample HIIT Workouts:

  • Bike sprints: 30 sec on, 90 sec off √ó 8 rounds
  • Bodyweight circuit: burpees, mountain climbers, jump squats
  • Treadmill intervals: incline walking/running

Yoga & Mind-Body Exercise

Mind-body exercises like yoga uniquely beneficial untuk PCOS, addressing both physical symptoms dan stress management.

Unique Benefits:

  • Reduces cortisol levels dan chronic stress
  • Improves body awareness dan self-acceptance
  • Enhances flexibility dan functional movement
  • Supports better sleep quality
  • Complements high-intensity training

Best Yoga Styles untuk PCOS:

Hatha, Vinyasa, dan Restorative yoga. Avoid overly heated environments yang dapat increase cortisol.

Recommended Practices:

  • Yoga 2-3x per week, 45-60 minutes
  • Pilates untuk core strength dan posture
  • Tai Chi untuk gentle movement dan mindfulness
  • Stretching routines untuk recovery

Important considerations untuk safe dan effective exercise dengan PCOS

Listen to Your Body

PCOS symptoms can fluctuate. Adjust intensity based pada energy levels, periode cycle, dan overall well-being. Rest is part of training.

Avoid Overtraining

Excessive exercise can increase cortisol dan worsen PCOS symptoms. Aim for balanced routine dengan adequate recovery time.

Monitor Blood Sugar

If you have insulin resistance atau diabetes, monitor blood glucose before/after exercise. Adjust medication timing dengan doctor guidance.

Stay Hydrated

PCOS dapat affect fluid balance. Drink water before, during, dan after exercise. Monitor urine color untuk hydration status.

Consult Healthcare Provider

Before starting new exercise program, especially jika ada complications seperti heart issues atau severe insulin resistance.

Gradual Progression

Increase intensity, duration, atau frequency gradually. Follow 10% rule: tidak lebih dari 10% increase per week.
