Prinsip Diet PCOS

Foundational principles untuk optimizing nutrition dan hormone balance dengan PCOS

📊

Low Glycemic Index

Pilih makanan dengan GI rendah untuk menjaga kadar gula darah stabil dan meningkatkan insulin sensitivity.

Mengapa GI Penting untuk PCOS:

Makanan GI rendah mengurangi insulin spikes, membantu weight management, dan memperbaiki ovulasi pada PCOS.

Pilihan GI Rendah (<55):

  • Quinoa, brown rice, oats
  • Sweet potato, legumes
  • Apel, berries, pear
  • Sayuran hijau dan non-starchy vegetables
  • Nuts dan seeds

Hindari GI Tinggi (>70):

  • White bread, white rice
  • Processed cereals, pastries
  • Soft drinks, candy
  • Instant noodles, crackers
🔥

Anti-Inflammatory Foods

PCOS sering associated dengan chronic inflammation. Anti-inflammatory diet dapat mengurangi symptoms dan memperbaiki metabolic health.

Powerful Anti-Inflammatory Foods:

  • Fatty fish (salmon, mackerel, sardines)
  • Berries (blueberries, strawberries)
  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Turmeric, ginger, garlic
  • Olive oil, avocado
  • Green tea, teh herbal

Inflammatory Foods to Limit:

Processed foods, refined sugars, trans fats, excessive omega-6 oils, dan alcohol dapat meningkatkan inflammation.

Herbs & Spices:

  • Cinnamon untuk insulin sensitivity
  • Spearmint tea untuk androgen reduction
  • Fenugreek untuk glucose control
⚖️

Portion Control & Timing

Meal timing dan portion control crucial untuk managing insulin levels dan supporting hormone balance.

Optimal Meal Timing:

  • 3 balanced meals + 1-2 healthy snacks
  • Tidak skip breakfast untuk metabolic stability
  • Larger meals di pagi hari, lighter di malam
  • Stop eating 3 jam sebelum tidur
  • Consistent meal times untuk hormone regulation

Plate Method untuk PCOS:

½ plate vegetables, ¼ lean protein, ¼ complex carbs, plus healthy fats seperti avocado atau nuts.

Portion Guidelines:

  • Protein: Palm-sized serving
  • Carbs: Cupped-hand portion
  • Fats: Thumb-sized amount
  • Vegetables: Unlimited non-starchy

Supplements untuk PCOS

Evidence-based supplements yang dapat mendukung PCOS management

💊

Inositol (Myo & D-Chiro)

Dosage: 2-4g myo-inositol + 100-200mg D-chiro daily

Gold standard supplement untuk PCOS. Inositol significantly improves insulin sensitivity, reduces androgen levels, dan supports regular ovulation.

  • Improves insulin sensitivity up to 70%
  • Reduces testosterone levels
  • Supports regular menstrual cycles
  • May improve fertility outcomes
  • Safe for long-term use
☀️

Vitamin D3

Dosage: 2000-4000 IU daily (test levels first)

Most PCOS women are deficient. Vitamin D deficiency worsens insulin resistance dan inflammation. Supplementation improves multiple PCOS parameters.

  • Enhances insulin sensitivity
  • Supports immune function
  • Improves bone health
  • May reduce inflammation
  • Supports mood regulation
🐟

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Dosage: 1-3g EPA/DHA daily with meals

Powerful anti-inflammatory supplement yang reduces chronic inflammation common dalam PCOS. Supports cardiovascular dan metabolic health.

  • Reduces chronic inflammation
  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Supports heart health
  • May reduce androgen levels
  • Supports brain health & mood
🌿

Spearmint Tea

Dosage: 2 cups daily (1-2g dried leaves)

Natural anti-androgen yang dapat help reduce hirsutism dan acne. Studies show significant reduction dalam free testosterone levels.

  • Reduces free testosterone
  • May improve hirsutism
  • Supports acne reduction
  • Natural & safe option
  • Antioxidant properties
🥄

Cinnamon Extract

Dosage: 500mg-1g daily or 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Ceylon cinnamon improves insulin sensitivity dan glucose metabolism. Natural way untuk support blood sugar control.

  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Supports glucose control
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • May reduce cravings
  • Antioxidant properties
💚

Magnesium

Dosage: 200-400mg daily (glycinate form preferred)

Essential mineral yang often deficient dalam PCOS. Supports insulin function, reduces inflammation, dan improves sleep quality.

  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Reduces chronic inflammation
  • Supports better sleep
  • Muscle relaxation & recovery
  • Stress reduction

⚠️ Important Supplement Notes

  • Always consult healthcare provider sebelum starting new supplements
